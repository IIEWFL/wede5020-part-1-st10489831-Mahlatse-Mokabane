About Us

"Where compassion finds a home"

Our Mission

We believe that everyone deserves compassion, safety, and respect. Shelter of Kindness exists to bridge the gap between short term crisis and long term recovery. By providing emergency refuge, resources, counseling, and skill development, we help people not only survive but rebuild their lives with hope and stability.

Our Vision

A community in which every person and family has access to safe housing, basic necessities, and opportunities for a brighter future.

Our Story

Founded in South Africa, Shelter of Kindness emerged as a response to rising challenge of homelessness and family displacement caused by unemployment and financial difficulties. What began as a small effort to provide meals and shelter has grown into a structured program supporting thousands across the region.

The purpose of our organization is to help close the gap between short-term crises and long term recovery. Our goal is to assist people in not just surviving but also rebuilding their lives with stability and hope by providing emergency refuge, necessary resources, counseling, and skill development.

Leadership

Our leadership team is committed to transparency, compassion, and community driven solutions. Together with dedicated staff and volunteers we work every day to make our vision a reality.

Who we Serve

  • Families in crisis due to eviction, domestic abuse, or financial hardship
  • Homeless individuals, including youth and the elderly
  • People with disabilities in underprivileged communities
