Emergency Shelter

Safe, temporary accommodation for families adn individuals facing homelessness, providing warmth, protection, and comfort during crisis.

  • Clean bedding, showers, and essential toiletries.
  • On-site support staff to connect residents with further resources.

Meal Programs

Nutritious daily meals served with dignity, ensuring that no one in our community goes hungry.

  • Hot, nutritious meals served daily.
  • Weekly community kitchen open to anyone in need.
  • Food parcel distribution for struggling households.

Outreach

Community based initiatives to connect with vulnerable groups, provide essential supplies, and promote awareness about homelessness.

  • Mobile teams deliver food, blankets, and hygiene packs to people living on the streets.
  • Awareness campaigns to educate communities about homelessness.
  • Partnerships with schools and clinics to identify at-risk families.

Family Support

Helping families feel in need.

  • Counselling for families recovering from trauma, eviction, or domestic challenges.
  • Skills development workshops (budgeting, parenting, career guidance).
  • Assistance with accessing government grants and social services.
